Our Story From Humble Beginnings to Sweet Success

Baker

Joy Nduta

Founder & Baker

What began as a small home bakery in 2010 has blossomed into Nairobi's premier destination for artisanal cakes and pastries. Chef Emma Richardson started SweetDelights with just one oven, a dream, and a passion for creating edible works of art.

Today, our team of 15 talented bakers and decorators continues this tradition of excellence, using only the finest locally-sourced ingredients and innovative techniques to craft cakes that delight both the eyes and the palate.
